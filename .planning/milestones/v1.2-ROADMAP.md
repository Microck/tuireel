## Milestone Summary

**Key Decisions:**

- Keep Tuireel PTY-native and deterministic; do not fake human feel with random jitter or browser-style motion tricks.
- Split raw capture cadence from delivery cadence through delivery profiles plus a persisted timing contract.
- Prove pacing, readability, and smoothness on exported artifacts, not only on structural proxies or verification notes.
- Carry Nyquist backfill forward as backlog cleanup instead of reopening shipped-scope requirements.

**Issues Resolved:**

- Ambiguity between capture cadence and output FPS.
- Robotic pacing and flat pause behavior in recorded flows.
- Weak 1080p readability defaults and under-proven motion smoothness.
- Missing artifact-backed diagnostics and acceptance proof for the shipped milestone claims.

**Issues Deferred:**

- Phase 36: Nyquist Backfill for Feature Phases.
- Phase 37: Nyquist Backfill for Acceptance Phases.

**Technical Debt Incurred:**

- Milestone archive generation still needed manual correction for shipped-scope stats and duplicate milestone entries.
- Nyquist validation coverage lagged behind shipped implementation and acceptance closure work.
